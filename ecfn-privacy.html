Privacy Statement for ECFN

Introduction

This Privacy Statement outlines the types of information that the ECFN app collects, how we use that information, and the choices users have regarding their personal information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: We collect and store information you provide when creating an account in ECFN. This includes your email address. Users can also use the app anonymously without creating an account.

Device and Usage Information: We collect information about your device and how you interact with the app, including the events you view and subscribe to.

Notifications: If you opt-in for notifications, we store your preferences to send you reminders and updates about Fortnite events on-device. This settings are not transfered to the cloud.

Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and improve user experience.

Ad Data: We use Google AdMob to display ads in ECFN. AdMob may collect and use information about your device and app usage to provide personalized advertising.

How We Use Your Information

To Provide Services: Your information is used to manage your account, provide event notifications, and ensure the app functions effectively. Our guiding approach is to store as less data as possible.

Improving User Experience: Analytics data helps us understand user behavior and preferences, enabling us to enhance the app.

Ad Serving: Google AdMob uses the information it collects to serve personalized ads within the app, enhancing your app experience with relevant content.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data collected through Firebase Analytics may be shared with Google in accordance with their privacy policies.

Data related to advertisements, including interactions with ads, may be shared with Google and other partners involved in the ad delivery process.

User Choices

You can choose to use ECFN anonymously. For account users, you can manage your notification preferences and account information within the app.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

However, no internet-based system can be 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Changes to this Privacy Statement

We may update our Privacy Statement periodically. We encourage users to review this statement regularly for any changes.
